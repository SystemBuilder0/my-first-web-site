goal of this path is for these individuals to vecome practical in their decision making.


In order to become more decisive they must first create the structure for arranging information relevant to a quesiton

so that the answer or course of action they seek is made clear. those on this path will learn to sift and weigh facts and data and learn how to analyze it by putting it into logical structures such as decision trees in order to help them make decisions. They must also work hard to learn how to create mental systems or heirarchies for ranking tasks or priorities. By recognizing that they have weak psychic boundaries and are frequently emotionally buffeted by the actions and feelings of others, those on this path can begin to create better internal and external structures to protect themselves from sensory overload.
